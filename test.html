Laboratory services SMM

Newsroom from scratch

Task: create social networks from scratch, fill them with content that stands out from other newsrooms, increase a live audience, as well as recognition among potential commercial partners
Actions:
• define the target audience
• created a strategy for brand development in the short and long term
• began to build up a pool of potential partners by interviewing them
• created a special visual style and content plan
• developed, edited and published the magazine as a editor-in-chief

Results:

Reached out to bloggers for advertising through barter, created high-quality content, monthly customer coverage more than 100K users in one year, launched a magazine, conducted 7 interviews, increased brand loyality
